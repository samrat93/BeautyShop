RETURN, REPLACEMENT AND REFUND POLICY

MyBeauty assures you the best of quality when it comes to our products. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason; you may return the product unused, and in the same condition as you received it, in its original packaging within 2 days from the date on which these goods are delivered. We shall issue 60% refund of the cost of the product (40% shall be charged as Order Processing charges,maintenance charges and other misc charges.)


Please note that we are not accepting any returns for customized stitched .cosmetic

Refund below 1000 INR will not be processed

You need to notify us first before returning any product. You may do this through Email Us or Call Us before returning any products. Returns made without notice SHALL NOT qualify for any kind of refund of payments.


You may return the product to us at mentioned address on contact us page.


Customer have to bear the shipping costs while returning the products mybeauty.com never pays the returning shipping cost in any case. [Applies to all countries]


In case of return of product. Your refund or product will be sent only after the product is received by us. We shall not refund the shipping charges paid by you while purchasing the item.


If we have products ready to get shipped at our premises at the time of refund request, customer will have to accept those products, refund will not be processed for it in any manner, and for remaining products it will be as per the refund policy which is 40% of order amount.


We shall not refund the customs duties or taxes, if applicable, or paid by you at time of receiving the goods. At any point of time, the refunds will not exceed the actual value of the goods.


Returns would not be accepted for custom made / stitched .cosmetic product

Any complaint with regards to the damage or stains or stitching or any other should be reported to us within 24 hrs of the receipt of the products. We may ask for digital picture of the same in order to review the same.


In case of product return, we will issue a refund once we receive the product back at our office.
